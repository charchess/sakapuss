## Overview

**Page Purpose:** Weight curve over time with trend indication. This is where data replaces worry. When the vet asks "since when?", Camille can show this screen directly. Anomalies are highlighted — unusual changes come to Camille, she does not have to interpret raw data.

**User Situation:** Camille tapped a weight entry in the timeline, or accessed from the animal profile. She wants to see: is Pixel gaining, stable, or losing? Since when? Is this concerning?

**Success Criteria:**
- Trend direction visible within 1 second (arrow + label)
- Time period switching without page reload
- Anomaly periods highlighted on the chart automatically
- Sharable — screenshot or "Share with vet" intent

**Entry Points:**
- Timeline weight entry tap (primary)
- Animal profile "Weight" section
- Anomaly banner tap from 04.1 (pre-focused on anomaly period)

**Exit Points:**
- Back → previous page (04.1 or 04.3 depending on entry)
- Tap "Share with vet" → 07.1 (future scenario)
- Bottom nav → Home, Timeline, Reminders, Profile

## Technical Notes

- **Chart library:** Use lightweight SVG-based chart (e.g., LayerCake for Svelte, or custom SVG). No heavy charting library — bundle size matters for PWA.
- **Trend calculation:** Linear regression over selected period. "Gaining" = slope > +0.5% per week. "Losing" = slope < -0.5% per week. "Stable" = within +/-0.5%.
- **Anomaly detection:** Same server-side algorithm as timeline banner. On this page, anomaly is visualized as a highlighted zone on the chart.
- **Period selector memory:** Last selected period is remembered per animal (localStorage).
- **Responsive chart:** Chart redraws on viewport change. On desktop, chart is wider with more x-axis labels. Landscape mode on mobile: chart expands to full width.
- **Data point density:** For periods > 3M, if many entries, thin data points to prevent overlapping. Show all points in tooltip on drag.
- **Share intent:** "Share with vet" generates a summary: chart screenshot + last 5 entries + trend. In v1, this navigates to 07.1 share flow. Future: direct PDF export.
- **Offline:** Chart renders from cached weight entries. Period selector works on local data.
